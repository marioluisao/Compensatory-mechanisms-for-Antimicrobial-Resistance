This folder contains the following data:

1. Transcriptional data of arcA deletion in aerobic and anaerobic conditions: normalized microarray data reported by Covert et al. 2004  (GEO accession : GSE1107) and the corresponding microarray probes to genes map
2. E. coli transcriptional network: derived from RegulonDB (http://regulondb.ccg.unam.mx/#)
3. Metabolic genes: list of genes included in the iJO1366 metabolic model of E. Coli
4. List of efflux pump genes
5. List of fermentation related genes
6. List of TCA related genes
